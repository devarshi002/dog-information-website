HUSKY

Siberian Huskies are medium-sized dogs bred for sled-pulling in harsh Arctic conditions. They have a wolf-like appearance, are friendly and gentle, require regular exercise, shed heavily, and may be prone to certain health issues. They need consistent training and a dedicated owner.
GERMAN SHEPHERD

The German Shepherd is a breed of medium to large-sized working dog that originated in Germany. They are known for their intelligence, strength, and loyalty, making them one of the most popular breeds for various roles including police and military work, search and rescue, therapy, and even as family pets. German Shepherds are highly trainable and are often used in roles that require obedience and agility. They have a double coat that is usually tan and black or red and black, and they require regular exercise and mental stimulation to stay happy and healthy.
LABRADOR RETRIEVER

The Labrador Retriever is a beloved breed known for its friendly demeanor and intelligence. They excel as family pets, therapy dogs, and in various working roles. Labs are skilled swimmers and have a gentle, loyal nature, making them a popular choice for many households.
